# Recruitment CRM Workflow

## Complete Recruitment Workflow

### Step 1 – Company Registration

An administrator creates a company account.

Company users can then access their dashboard.

---

### Step 2 – Job Creation

The company creates a job posting.

Example:

Company:
ABC Recruitment

Job:
Backend Developer

Status:
Open

---

### Step 3 – Candidate Registration

A candidate creates an account or logs in using Google OAuth.

The candidate completes their profile and uploads a CV.

---

### Step 4 – Job Application

The candidate applies for a job.

A new application record is created.

Example:

John Smith
→ Backend Developer
→ Applied

---

### Step 5 – Application Review

The company reviews incoming applications.

Applications move through recruitment stages.

### Available Stages

- Applied
- Screening
- Shortlisted
- Interview Scheduled
- Interview Done
- Evaluated
- Offer Sent
- Hired
- Rejected

---

### Step 6 – Interview Scheduling

The company schedules an interview.

The candidate automatically receives a notification.

---

### Step 7 – Evaluation

Recruiters evaluate candidates after interviews.

Evaluation records include:

- Score
- Recommendation
- Feedback

---

### Step 8 – Activity Management

Recruiters create follow-up activities.

Examples:

- Candidate Follow-Up
- Interview Reminder
- Offer Discussion

---

### Step 9 – Notifications

Automatic notifications are generated when:

- A candidate applies
- An interview is scheduled
- An application status changes

---

### Step 10 – Hiring Decision

The company updates the final result.

Possible outcomes:

- Hired
- Rejected

The recruitment cycle is completed.

---
