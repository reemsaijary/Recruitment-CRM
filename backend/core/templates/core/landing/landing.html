How It Works

From company access to successful hiring

HireHub follows a clear workflow where companies are onboarded by the admin, candidates apply with Google login, and hiring teams manage the full process.

01

Company Requests Access

Companies contact HireHub to join the recruitment platform.

02

Admin Creates Account

The admin creates company accounts and controls platform access.

03

Companies Post Jobs

Companies publish job openings and manage recruitment needs.

04

Candidates Apply

Candidates sign in with Google, browse jobs, and apply easily.

05

Interviews Are Scheduled

Companies review applications and schedule interviews with candidates.

06

Top Talent Gets Hired

The hiring process ends with organized decisions and candidate updates.
